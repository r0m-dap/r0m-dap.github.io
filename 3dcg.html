Here you can see images and animations with results of my activity in three-dimensional computer graphics sphere.

I am developing my skills in high- & low-poly 3d modeling, 3d animation and photorealistic rendering.

My primary software weapon is Maya. I like this 3dcg program very much and always trying to realize my professional tasks using Maya.

But many ways of modern development mechanisms forced me to use much more 3dcg software programs like 3DSMax, ZBrush, SketchUp, Poser, Blender and many more less complex 3dcg tools.

To achieve success in photorealistic rendering tasks i am resorting to force of 2D photo & video editing programs like Photoshop, Gimp, PaintShop, CorelDraw, AfterEffects, Shake etc.

I am always looking for 3dcg software scene to be ready for using new programs if they gives to me unique possibilities.
