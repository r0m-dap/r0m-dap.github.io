Pixelart is magic world for me. It is not only sphere of my professional activities but also my favorite toy!

I have some years experience in games development as pixelartist/designer of graphical content for applications on mobile phones and other handheld devices.

Some examples of my 16-bit pixelart can be found lower on this page.

Most of all i like to set & reset pixels formatted for oldschool 8-bit computers like ZX-Spectrum.

8-bit pixelart for me closely associated to my activity on demoscene and some of my pictures was made especially for demos.

Many pictures was dedicated to competitions at demoscene parties where it get many prized places.

Demoscene products with my participation, mostly as graphican/designer, and all 8-bit pixelart screens can be found at PROGRESS demogroup web site.

Also most of my and many others pixelart can be reached at big demoscene art web resource - ArtCity.
